BRONCO-SPECIFIC NOTES

  • Marine vinyl seats: Sasquatch and Wildtrak trims come with marine-grade vinyl. You still benefit from Cordura covers — vinyl handles water but not UV or abrasion as well.
  • Removable doors = rain: When doors come off, rain gets in. Cordura dries faster than neoprene. PIAA-grade covers handle moisture best.
  • Bronco Sport: Different vehicle entirely. These covers do NOT fit the Bronco Sport. Search for Bronco Sport covers on Amazon.
