The Gladiator combines Wrangler trail capability with a usable truck bed — making it the ultimate work-and-play vehicle. But that versatility means the seats take double abuse: trail dirt on the weekends, job site grime during the week. Tactical covers aren’t optional here.

GLADIATOR VS WRANGLER JLU — WHAT FITS?

Critical info: the Gladiator shares front seats with the Wrangler JLU, but the rear bench is different. The Gladiator’s rear is shallower due to the shorter cab. If you buy JLU rear covers for your Gladiator, they’ll bunch up and look terrible. Always buy Gladiator-specific rear covers.
