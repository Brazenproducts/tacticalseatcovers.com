JK vs JL — Does It Matter?

Yes. The JK (2007–2018) and JL (2018+) have different seat bolt patterns, airbag locations, and headrest mounting. Always buy generation-specific covers. Bartact has separate patterns for both — Coverking does too, but Smittybilt uses one universal pattern for both.
